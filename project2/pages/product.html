Farmin' Marvin's Produce

Here’s a taste of what Marvin grows and sells with his signature charm. Every item is farmed by hand and chosen at peak freshness — nothing store-bought here!

  • Tomatoes – Juicy, sun-kissed, and just the right balance of sweet and acidic.
  • Onions – Bold and flavorful, perfect for cooking or slicing raw for that extra kick.
  • Beets – Earthy and vibrant, they roast up like candy (well, veggie candy).
  • Delphinium – Tall, majestic flowers with cool colors that steal the show in any bouquet.
  • Zinnias – Bold, bright, and cheerful blooms that last long in a vase.
  • Lithiansis – Delicate and whimsical, adding a soft touch to floral arrangements.
  • Watermelon – Heavy, sweet, and juicy enough to cause a happy mess.
  • Peaches – Fuzzy, fragrant, and bursting with late-summer sunshine in every bite.
  • Strawberries – Red gems that taste like the first day of summer.

Everything Marvin sells is grown with care, harvested with love, and sure to bring a little green goodness into your life.
