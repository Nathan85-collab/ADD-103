The best things of Christmas

Christmas vibes

The feeling of staying inside and being next to a warm fire is unmatched by any other time of the year. If it weren't for the Christmas holiday, winter would suck the entire season. Look and january for example, after new years, the entire month is just a frozen wasteland.

Christmas decorations

Christmas decor makes winter pop with color and happiness. The creaitvity used to make houses and towns look christmas themed makes going outside worth it, despite the 10 degree weather.

Christmas views

Snow on the ground is so oddly satisfying as it blankets the ground and trees. It is very blinding when the sun comes out, but still very nice to look at.
